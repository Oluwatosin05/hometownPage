Visit Lagos

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of the state

Top three activities to do in Lagos

Go for a picnic at the park

JJT park is a place for picnic and get together for friends and family. I love the atmosphere, the free air and art work, the fountain and the water fall.

Attend a concert or a show

The National Theatre, Nigeria is the primary centre for the performing arts in Nigeria. The monument is located in Iganmu, Surulere, Lagos.

Get sweaty with the sport field

The Lagos National Stadium is a multi-purpose stadium in Surulere, Lagos State, Nigeria, which comprises an Olympic-size swimming arena and a multipurpose arena
